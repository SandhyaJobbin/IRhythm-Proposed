1

Download the App

You can download the MyZio app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

App Store Google Play
2

Log in/Sign up

  • Click on 'Create Account' to create a new account
  • Enter your details [name, email, phone number] and set a password
  • You can also log into your existing account using the 'Log in' option
3

Register Your Zio Monitor

  • Click on 'Register Monitor' and enter the serial number on your monitor
  • Confirm the start date/time when you applied the monitor
4

Logging Your Symptoms

If you experience anything unusual:

  • Press the button on your Zio monitor
  • Tap on 'Log Symptom' in the MyZio app
  • Enter:
    • What you felt
    • Start time
    • Duration
    • What you were doing
5

Tracking Monitor Status

The app shows you:

  • How long the device has been worn
  • Instructions for removal/returns
  • Help links and FAQs
6

Returning the Device

Once you complete your prescribed wear time:

  • Tap 'Finish' in the app
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to return the device

Important Medical Notice

If you need immediate medical attention, call 911

The Zio monitor does not provide real-time medical assistance or contact emergency services automatically.
