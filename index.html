1

Wear Your Zio Monitor

Your healthcare provider will prescribe a Zio monitor to help track your heart's rhythm continuously.

  • Monitor can be applied:
    • At your doctor's office
    • Self-applied at home using provided instructions
2

Track Your Symptoms

When you experience unusual symptoms, it's important to log them for accurate diagnosis.

If you experience any unusual symptoms like
Dizziness Chest Pain Palpitations
  • Press the top of the Zio patch to log your symptoms.
  • You can also record your symptom descriptions in the provided symptom log booklet or use the MyZio app.
3

Return Your Monitor

After completing your prescribed wear time, safely return your monitor for analysis.

  • Place the monitor and the log booklet in the original box.
  • Seal and mail the box through any USPS mailbox.
4

Understand Your Data

  • iRhythm will review your Zio monitor data and create a clear, comprehensive report based on the analysis.
  • This report can help your healthcare providers to develop a personalized care plan.
